UVU Course Support

Welcome to Your Course!

The Service Desk is dedicated to providing quality support and services to our students, faculty, and staff.
One role of the Service Desk is to provide technical assistance to students and instructors throughouts the semester.

Please be aware that the Service Desk cannot provide assistance with homework, coursework or mentoring, but we would be happy to connect you with a variety of campus resources, such as tutoring and student success.
